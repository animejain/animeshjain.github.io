Extra Curricular Activities

Awaaz, the campus media body of IIT Kharagpur

Chief Editor | August 2018 - Present

As a media body of IIT Kharagpur, we have reported on an astonishingly wide spectrum of topics spanning over administrative issues, academic programs, industrial outlook, students' activties, research highlights, college politics and alumni talks.

We reach the public through our Facebook Page(having 37k followers), Website, Medium Blogs, our annual newsletter and other social platforms. I joined Awaaz as a Junior Reporter in my 1st year when I worked on ground on events like the General Championship and other events. In the later years when I was given leading positions, I worked on various initiatives like the Micro-Specialization Series and Reviewing Online Semester, both tuning into the academic compass and some sensitive issues like Post-Covid Reopening of Campus and Lobbying in Campus Politics; both recieving high traction in the students' community. Apart from these, I interviewed and led the work on articles like Entrepreneurship and Incubation, Smart India Hackathon, Jewels of KGP etc.

As an Editor and now as a Chief Editor, I have been mentoring the junior batches over the same line of articles which I have seen getting better in every aspect being in their hands. Awaaz's efforts have always been lauded by the student community and faculty.
